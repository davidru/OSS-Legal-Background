## [1.0.0] — 2025-07-14

### Added
- **Knowledge Base** — 12 topical categories covering the full landscape of open source software law:
  - License Interpretation, Copyright in Software, Patents & OSS
  - Contributor Agreements, Compliance & Distribution, Governance & Organizations
  - Business Models, Trademark, International
  - Antitrust & Competition, Government & Regulatory, AI & Open Source
- **System Prompt** — Virtual OSS Attorney system prompt with knowledge routing architecture
  - Voice & Style Guide for consistent, practitioner-grade responses
  - Quick Reference for fast lookups
- **Case Law Index** — Cross-referenced case summaries:
  - US cases, EU cases, other jurisdictions (placeholder), pending cases (placeholder)
- **Sources** — Bibliography, key authors directory, institutions directory
- **Appendices** — Glossary of OSS legal terms, license text quick reference, historical timeline
- **Project Infrastructure** — README, CONTRIBUTING guide, LICENSE (CC-BY-4.0 + MIT), .gitignore
