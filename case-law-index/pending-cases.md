## How to Contribute

Track a pending case by submitting a PR with:
1. Full case citation and court
2. Current procedural status
3. Key OSS law issues at stake
4. Brief explanation of potential impact
5. Link to docket or publicly available filings

When a case reaches final disposition, move it to the appropriate jurisdiction file ([us-cases.md](us-cases.md), [eu-cases.md](eu-cases.md), or [other-jurisdictions.md](other-jurisdictions.md)).
